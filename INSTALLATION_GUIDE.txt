Installation of codychat
---------------------------

step 1 - Make sure your server meet requirements before attempting to install.

step 2 - Create a empty database on your server.

step 3 - Upload content of " Codychat " folder to your desired location on your server

step 4 - In your desired browser reach the root url where you have uploaded files.

step 5 - follow installer and provide license key.

step 6 - Installation complete.
